about us

A coffee bean is the seed of the coffee plant. It's the part that gets roasted, and ground into the coffee granules you use to brew your cup of NESCAFÉ every day. Often referred to as a cherry, it is the pip inside the red or purple fruit of the coffee plant



our story

The first café is said to have opened in 1550 in Constantinople; during the 17th century cafés opened in Italy, France, Germany, and England. During the 200 years after the mid-17th century,the most famous coffeehouses of Europe flourished in London as ready points for news, discussion, and faction.



our history

Today, Bean & Brew stands as a testament to James and Sarah's vision and the enduring power of coffee to bring people together. Though they have since passed the torch to a new generation of coffee enthusiasts, their legacy lives on in every sip and every smile shared within our walls.
